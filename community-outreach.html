Community Outreach

Nyanga Small Scale Timber Producers

In Manicaland so far 18 groups were issued with permits for timber production and this has seen 148 being employed. The timber processed is sold throughout the country by supplying individuals, retailers and wholesalers thereby contributing to the economy of the Republic of Zimbabwe.

Mutarazi Community Curio Shop

A total of 140 families have organised themselves into groups to run a Mutarazi community curio shop and these have been trained in leadership/ conflict management, financial literacy and constitution drafting.
