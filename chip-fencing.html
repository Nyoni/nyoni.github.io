Fencing Programmes

In many of our remote and most pristine protected areas, people have co-existed with wildlife Game fencing helps to minimize the risk of animals leaving the park into communities, residential areas or highways where they become increasingly vulnerable. by choice, but by necessity.

Maintaining our fences is therefore critical to the welfare of both man and beast, these barriers ensure that human wildlife conflict is minimized and also limit the possibility of poaching, especially where endangered species like the rhino are involved.
