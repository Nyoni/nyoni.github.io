Research Strategy

The Zimbabwe Parks and Wildlife Authority's Research strategy focuses on the following long and short-term goals.


Long term Goal

To contribute to the conservation of biodiversity and sustainable use of wildlife in Zimbabwe by conducting exemplary research and providing scientific information and advice to policy makers, resource managers, stakeholders, and the public

Short term Goal

To develop and implement a research strategy that covers 3 areas.

  1. Provide science-based information on wildlife conservation and management options to support the implementation of the Environmental Management Act, the Parks and Wild Life Act, the National Biodiversity Strategic Action Plan, and other relevant provisions;
  2. Focus and coordinate wildlife research in Zimbabwe on key wildlife conservation and management issues; and
  3. Build wildlife research capacity in the ZPWMA and associated research partners in the country
