Preserving our natural heritage is an enormous task that needs “all paws on deck.” From the evils of the illegal wildlife trade, to pollution, poaching and ever increasing pressure on our planet, a combined effort is needed to counter or at least limit the effects of these threats. Navigating this website will open your eyes to the many processes that occur behind the scenes to collectively support the protection of our beloved animals. Some are endangered while others are threatened by loss of habitat though climate change and other adversities.

We acknowledge our role in advancing the attainment of sustainable development goals in 4 key areas

Zimparks invites you to Chip in with resources or time and play your part in protecting our planet together!
