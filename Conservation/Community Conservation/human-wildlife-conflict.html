Roles

The role of Community Conservation Programs in HWC quite broad. This involves physically interact with: members of parliament, Councillorâ€™s, Traditional leaders, rural communities, NGOs, Civil society, private wildlife farmers and other relevant ministries at the grass-root level.

Functions

  • Community education awareness creation, mobilization & extension services.
  • Establish practical mechanism for benefit sharing with relevant stakeholders through consultation, negotiation and consensus building;
  • Offering wildlife technical services to private landowners, communities and Rural District Councils etc;
  • Providing required support for community (social & enterprise) projects;
  • Establishing mechanism to minimize conflicts between animals and humans.
  • To protect people and their property from injury or damage caused by wildlife.

Causes of human/ wildlife conflict

  • Lack of proper land use planning.
  • Encroachment into protected areas.
  • Population increase.
  • Poaching.

Types of human/wildlife conflict

  • Human injury
  • Human death
  • Crop damage
  • Property damage
  • Livestock predation
  • Human threats
