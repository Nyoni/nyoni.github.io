Private Wildlife Farmers

Zimbabwe Parks and Wildlife Management Authority promotes wildlife farming by individuals who wish to turn their unproductive crop production land into wildlife farming. Wildlife farming do have various advantages. Wildlife farming is concerned with the utilization of a wide spectrum of wild species, which is mostly conducted on extensive areas.

It operates with lower input and management costs because native animals are better adapted to local environmental conditions, including heat and drought, and have greater resistance to diseases, parasites and toxic plants.

Habitat degradation is a problem throughout many African grazing lands, but game animals can be kept at high stocking density because the mix of browsers and grazers makes optimal use of all vegetation types. Furthermore, game animals tend to be more mobile than livestock, and are not as tied to watering locations, thus achieving better distribution across landscape which reduces the potential for overgrazing. It also have high economic return.

Community livelihoods

Zimparks is engaged into various community livelihoods projects. In the move to empower the communities to venture into self-sustaining projects, community people have been training in fish farming projects covering various topics which includes site selection, pond construction, fish health, feeding and biosecurity measures among other topics

  1. In Manicaland so far 18 groups were issued with permits for timber production (Nyanga Small Scale Timber producers) and this has seen 148 being employed. The timber processed is sold throughout the country by supplying individuals, retailers and wholesalers thereby contributing to the economy of the Republic of Zimbabwe.
  2. A total of 140 families have organised themselves into groups to run a Mutarazi Community Curio shop and these have been trained in leadership/ conflict management, financial literacy and constitution drafting.
  3. Tshabalala Game Sanctuary Curio shop – Mat South Province
  4. Matobo community Curio shop – Mat South Province
